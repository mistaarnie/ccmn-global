2016 CCMN International Short-Term Missions

CCMN stands for "Cell Church Missions Network".

CCMN was established in 1996, which was formed by churches from different regions as a network. Its vision is to complete the Great Commission of God. The main purpose of CCMN, and the whole reason it exists, is to facilitate relationships between churches who want to network to do missions.
